The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to africa and india. It has a muscular,broad-chested body; a short, rounded head; round ears; and a dark, hairy tuft at the tip of its tail.



Eagles are not a natural group but denote essentially any kind of bird of prey large enough to hunt sizeable(about 50cm long or more overall) vertebrates.



Most species of snake are nonvenomous and those that have venom use it primarily to kill and subdue prey rather than for self-defense.
